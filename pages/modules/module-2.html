How to Navigate This Module

Be sure to complete the assignments in the order listed! Begin each lab by reading through the lab exercise on your own. Once you are familiar with the material, you can complete the assignments more effectively.

Within each module, you will find lab exercises (some labs have two parts) and lab quizzes. Some modules also include videos to support your studies.

  • Complete each Lab Exercise (some have two parts).
  • Complete each Lab Quiz, repeating up to three times if desired to improve your score and confidence.
  • Take the Lab Exam that covers material from all labs in this module.
