Synopsis

This week moves from taxonomy and phylogeny into major groups of eukaryotic organisms. You will begin with protists, a highly diverse group of mostly microscopic eukaryotes, then move into plant diversity by comparing mosses, ferns, gymnosperms, and angiosperms. The goal is to recognize major organismal groups, identify key structural differences, and connect those differences to life cycles, reproduction, and adaptation to land.
